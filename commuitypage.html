About Us

Kolej Shah Alam is a leading educational institution in Shah Alam, Malaysia. Established in 2010, we have been providing high-quality education to students from all over the world. Our experienced and dedicated faculty members ensure that our students receive the best possible education.

We offer a wide range of courses, including diploma, degree, and postgraduate programs. Our courses are designed to equip our students with the skills and knowledge they need to excel in their chosen fields.
